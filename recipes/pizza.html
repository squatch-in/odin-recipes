Pizza

Description

Its pizza of course. We dont need to over complacate things. Add your sauce to some dough and throw some cheese on there somewhere and BAMyou og yourself a pizza

WARNING: adding topping may increase your coolness factore by 10.

Ingreadents
  • Dough
  • Sause
  • Cheese
Instructions
  1. Set oven to 425
  2. Add dough to pizza pan
  3. Add sause of your choosing to dough. ammount is up to you if you like it sausey- add some more
  4. Add cheese on top of sause
  5. Put pizza in oven and now wait 12-20 min for the magic to happen
  6. Take pizza out and enjoy
