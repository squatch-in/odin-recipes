Lasagna

Ingredients

---------------

For the Meat Sauce:
  • 1 lb (450 g) ground beef (or half beef, half Italian sausage)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz / 800 g) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz / 170 g) cans tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp tomato sauce or water (if needed for consistency)
  • 2 tsp sugar (optional, balances acidity)
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt & pepper to taste
For the Cheese Mixture:
  • 15 oz (425 g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley (fresh or dried)
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
Other Ingredients:
  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Extra Parmesan for topping

Instructions

---------------

Cook the meat sause
  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef (and sausage if using) over medium heat until browned.
  2. Add onion and garlic; sauté until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, herbs, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  4. Simmer for 20–30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
Prepare the cheese layer
  1. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, parsley, and Parmesan. Stir until smooth and creamy.
Cook the Noodles (if not oven-ready):
  1. Boil in salted water until al dente. Drain and lay flat on a baking sheet to prevent sticking.
Assemble the lasagna
  • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  • In a 9×13-inch (23×33 cm) baking dish:
    1. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom.
    2. Add a layer of noodles.
    3. Spread some ricotta mixture.
    4. Sprinkle mozzarella.
    5. Repeat layers until ingredients are used up, finishing with sauce and mozzarella on top.
    6. Sprinkle extra Parmesan.
Bake
  • Cover with foil (lightly greased so it doesn’t stick to cheese).
  • Bake 25 minutes covered, then 20 minutes uncovered until bubbling and golden.
  • Let rest 15 minutes before slicing. This helps it hold its shape.
