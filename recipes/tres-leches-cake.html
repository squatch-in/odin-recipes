Tres Leches Cake

Description

Want a dessert that is both moist and rich in flavor;
Then look no further then a Tres Leches Cake.

Ingreadents

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
    • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 ½ cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Sift flour and baking powder together.
  3. Using a electric mixer mix together the suger and butter, add eggs and mix real good. Then add the flour then mix till well blended.
  4. Add batter into pan,and place into oven.
  5. Wait till cake edges are browning and by sticking a butter knife in the middle doesnt leave any batter residue.
  6. Add small holes into the top of your cake using a fork, and now wait 30 min to let cake cool.
  7. While we wait, mix the "tres leches"(whole milk, condensed milk, evaporated milk) on top of your cake and let the cake do the magic.
  8. As the milk is soaking into the cake we will make our cool whip. this is simply made by taking your heavy whipping cream adding some powder suger and mixing it with the electric mixer till you get soft peaks.
  9. Now add the cool whip on top and spread evenly.
  10. Add and fruit you would like and bam, thats a tres leches cake.

*In my opinion tres leches cake always tastes better after you let it sit in the fridge for a couple hours.
